About the Project

I've always been obsessed with the idea of having my own AI assistant, so I started building Jarvis 2.0. It's a Python-based desktop assistant that uses Gemini 2.5 Flash to handle voice commands and "see" my screen in real-time.

It’s far from finished, but I’m constantly pushing updates to make it faster and more capable. This is my attempt at bringing a bit of sci-fi into the real world.
